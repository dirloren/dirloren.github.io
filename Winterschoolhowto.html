How to Winter School on Convex Optimization

The winter school will use different tools for different parts of the program.

  • The lectures will be under the link https://webconf.tu-bs.de/dir-227-hvp (access code 936849). The link will you lead to a conference room in Big Blue Button.
  • The social part of the school as well as the poster presentation will happen in gather.town under the link https://gather.town/app/RUvU3CMbUgnNyQDk/ITNWinterSchool2021. The gather space will be open during the whole school and can be used at any time for meetings of any size.
  • A small “speed dating” to get to know each other will be using glimpse
  • For the members of the ITN “TraDE-OPT” there will be a special day on entrepreneurship, organized by the Entrepeneurship Hub. This day will be Wednesday and this event will be run using Webex. More information on this event will be sent to the members of the ITN directly via email.

Info on tools:

Big Blue Button

Big Blue Button is a conference system that runs in your browser. It is known to work well with Chrome and Firefox (and issues have been reported with Edge and Safari, for example) and basically all devices (including computers, laptops, tablets, and mobile phones). One you enter the room you will be asked for permission to connect to audio and then you can start to connect to video with the big blue button on the bottom of the space. The system is pretty intuitive and similar to many other systems.

Gather.town

Gathe.town allows you to move around in a virtual space and getting in contact with people. You just follow the link, give permissions for audio and video and you can start to move around with your arrow keys. The system needs a laptop or a computer and does not work well on mobile phones or tablets.

Glimpse

Glimpse is a social platform for back-to-back, 1-on-1 video chats. They guarantee the best matches through their unique tagging system. Whether it’s new member orientation, happy hour with best friends, or speed networking at a conference, Glimpse keeps your community engaged, friendly, and connected.

My Glimpse video isn't working, what can I do?

Make sure you turn off your video on Zoom, Google Meet, or MS Teams and try again. Typically, this issue arises because computers running Windows don't allow two video screens at the same time.

Browsers/Devices Supported

Web

Google Chrome: Recommended

Safari, Edge, and Firefox: Glimpse works, however these are not tested as rigorously.

Mobile

Glimpse is available on iOS and Android by installing our apps.

You cannot use Glimpse Web in your mobile browser.

Webex

Webex runs on all systems but with different utilities. There are apps available for Window, iOS and Android and under Linux it runs directly in the browser.
